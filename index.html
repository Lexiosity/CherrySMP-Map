CherrySMP Maps is your go-to resource for exploring different maps of each world in our SMP server, CherrySMP. Whether you're a player on the server or just curious about our world, you can use these maps to navigate and discover various locations.

How to Use CherrySMP Maps

  1. Choose the world you want to explore from the navigation menu.
  2. Explore the map using the interactive features to zoom in, zoom out, and pan around.
  3. Discover key landmarks, player builds, and other interesting locations.

Enjoy your journey through the CherrySMP worlds, and feel free to reach out if you have any questions or feedback!
